About Loyalist Exploration

Company Profile

Loyalist Exploration is a Canadian mineral exploration company focused on acquiring, exploring, and developing high-potential mineral properties in the Timmins Mining District. Our current flagship assets include the Gold Rush property and the Loveland Nickel-Copper-Gold property.

We are driven by a commitment to transparency, sustainability, and delivering long-term value to our investors, stakeholders, and communities.
